# Privacy Policy

_Last updated: March 15, 2025_

We take the privacy of everyone who relies on Turistar to plan memorable trips seriously. This policy explains which data we
collect, why we need it, and how you can control your information.

## 1. Information we collect

We gather data to deliver a personalized, safe, and reliable experience.

- **Account details:** name, email address, profile photo, and preferences provided during sign-up.
- **Planned content:** itineraries, destinations, notes, photos, and any other information added to travel boards.
- **Product usage:** interaction events, customized settings, feedback, and technical logs that help us diagnose issues.
- **Optional information:** additional details you voluntarily share in surveys or product experiments.

## 2. How we use data

We process data solely for legitimate purposes aligned with the service.

1. Deliver and improve travel-planning features.
2. Provide customer support and communicate relevant updates.
3. Ensure security, detect abuse, and prevent fraud.
4. Personalize recommendations for itineraries, accommodations, and activities.
5. Comply with legal obligations and respond to authorities when required.

## 3. Data sharing

We never sell personal data. We only share information in the situations below:

- **Trusted vendors:** partners that help with hosting, performance analytics, or support—always under confidentiality and
  security obligations.
- **Authorized integrations:** services you connect yourself (for example, calendars) that receive only the data required to
  operate.
- **Legal requirements:** when law or a court order compels us to disclose information.

## 4. Your controls and choices

- Access, update, or delete your account data directly in the app.
- Export itineraries and planned content in compatible formats.
- Request full account removal through the dedicated support channel.
- Adjust preferences for transactional emails at any time.

## 5. Retention and security

We store data only as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes described in this policy. Turistar uses encryption at rest and
in transit, environment segregation, and continuous monitoring to prevent unauthorized access.

## 6. International transfers

When we transfer data outside your country, we apply appropriate safeguards—such as standard contractual clauses—to ensure an
equivalent level of protection.

## 7. Updates to this policy

We may update this policy to reflect product improvements or legal requirements. We will communicate material changes with
reasonable advance notice.

## 8. Contact

For questions or requests related to privacy, reach out to [privacy@turistar.app](mailto:privacy@turistar.app).
